Email Sending Policy

This policy explains how aleksaitcyber uses verified transactional email services (currently Mailgun, previously Amazon SES) for infrastructure-related communication.”

Purpose of Email Sending

Effective Date: October 2025

We send transactional system notifications only. These include:

  • Server health alerts
  • Backup and snapshot status
  • Security notifications (SSH login, fail2ban events)
  • System uptime and monitoring alerts

No Unsolicited Email

We do not send:

  • Marketing or newsletters
  • Cold outreach or bulk email
  • Third-party or purchased email lists

Sender Authentication

Outgoing mail is authenticated using:

  • DKIM signing (enabled through Mailgun)
  • Custom MAIL FROM domain (mail.aleksaitcyber.com)
  • SPF record including Mailgun
  • DMARC policy enabled at domain level

Receiving & Monitoring

Transactional mail is sent from:

alerts@mail.aleksaitcyber.com

We monitor bounces and complaints and take corrective action immediately.

All messages are authenticated, signed, and rate-limited. We do not provide third-party relay or forwarding for other domains.

All email operations are internal and authenticated under mail.aleksaitcyber.com.

Abuse Contact

If you believe you received unintended email from our systems, contact:

contact@aleksaitcyber.com
